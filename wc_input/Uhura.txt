Nyota Uhura is a 
character in Star Trek: The Original Series, Star Trek: 
The Animated Series, the first six 
Star 
Trek 
films, the 2009 film Star Trek, 
and its 2013 sequel Star Trek Into Darkness. The character was portrayed by
 Nichelle 
Nichols in all but the most recent two Star Trek films, in which 
a younger Uhura was portrayed by actress Zoë Saldana.

Uhura was an important part of the 
original 
series' multicultural crew[1] 
and one of the first characters of African descent to be featured in a 
non-menial role on an American television 
series.
