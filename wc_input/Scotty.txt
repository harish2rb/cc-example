
Montgomery Scott, better known as 
"Scotty", is a 
fictional character in the Star 
Trek science fiction franchise.[1] First portrayed by James Doohan in the original 
Star Trek series, Scotty 
also appears in the animated Star Trek series, seven Star 
Trek movies, the Star Trek: The Next 
Generation episode "Relics", 

and in numerous books, 

comics, and video games.
