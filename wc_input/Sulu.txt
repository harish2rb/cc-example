Hikaru 
Sulu is a character in the Star Trek media franchise.[1] 
Originally known simply as 'Sulu', he was portrayed by George
 Takei in the original Star Trek series. 
Sulu also appears in the 
animated Star Trek series, the first 
six Star 
Trek movies, one episode 
of Star Trek: Voyager, and in numerous books, comics, and video games.[2] 
Sulu having the first name 
'Hikaru' appeared in a 1981 novel, well over a 
decade after the original series had ended. 
John Cho assumed the role of 
the character in both the 2009 film Star 
Trek[3] and its sequel, Star Trek 
Into Darkness.
